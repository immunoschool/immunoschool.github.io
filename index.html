Description

This intensive international summer course is designed to provide students, fellows, academics and professionals with the means to understand key concepts and cutting-edge advances in the design of current immunotherapy strategies.

We have gathered exceptional immunology and immunotherapy experts throughout the world to discuss novel perspectives in immunotherapy, review the extraordinary development of novel technology to map immune lesions and discuss the transformative impact of immunomodulatory strategies in the treatment of human cancer, inflammatory diseases and infectious diseases.
